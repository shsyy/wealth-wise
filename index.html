Welcome to WealthWise Financial Tracker

Take control of your finances with WealthWise. Track your income and expenses, categorize transactions, and view summaries. Here's what you can do:

  • Add Transactions: Log your income and expenses with details like amount, category, and date.
  • View Transactions: See a summary of all your financial activities in an organized table.
  • Summarize Finances: View total income and expenses for weekly or monthly periods.
  • Categorize Expenses: Group your expenses into categories (e.g., food, rent, utilities).
  • Edit/Delete Transactions: Update or remove any transaction easily.
  • Search & Filter: Find specific transactions using search and filter options.
